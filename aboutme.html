About Me

More than an analyst

Background

Hello! As stated on the home page, I am Uways (pronounced oh-ways). I was born in Chicago, IL to Pakistani immigrants and shortly thereafter moved to Houston, TX, where I would grow up. I am proud to be a first-generation college student who graduated with high honors from the University of Texas at Austin. After an unorthodox career path, I am looking to work full-time in the data space. I proudly consider myself a life-long learner as I love to learn about new subjects, explore new passions, and strive for self-improvement in both my personal and professional life.

Basketball

I am a massive NBA fan! I always loved basketball growing up, whether it was playing with kids in my neighborhood or watching high-flying dunks on national TV, but over the last few years my love for hoops has grown exponentially as I've developed a real passion for learning more about the sport and what cultivates success. This comes in the form of broadening my knowledge of sets and schemes, exploring advanced team and player statistics, as well as evaluating prospective draft selections to develop my own opinions on team building and what impacts winning. Nowadays my love for basketball has extended to the WNBA, as well as college and overseas leagues and I continue to be excited by how player skillsets and on-court strategy develop over time.

Cooking

I consider myself a foodie, and what better way to enjoy food you love than making it in the comfort of your own home and customizing it to your preferences. I've thoroughly enjoyed learning how to cook various cuisines and exploring new techniques. My favorite dishes to cook as of late are thai red curry, kimchi fried rice, and crawfish étouffée.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding is a newer passion of mine! I tried it for the first time in 2022 and have fallen in love. While there is a steep learning curve, I've had an absolute blast progressing my skills and I look forward to continuing to get more comfortable on tougher terrain. Gliding down a mountain is an ethereal experience and having snowboarded a few times now, I can confidently say it will never get old.

Milo and Elliot

Meet my sons! Milo and El. I adopted them in 2022 and it was truly one of the best decisions I've ever made. They bring an immense amount of laughter and joy to me and my fiancée's life and I can no longer imagine my life without them. They are brothers (by circumstance) and best friends (by choice) and they remind me to not take life too seriously and to always stay curious.
